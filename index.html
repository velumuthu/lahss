About Our School

Lourdu Annai Higher School stands as a beacon of educational excellence, dedicated to providing quality education from Class 1 to Class 12. Our institution combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies to create a nurturing environment where every student can thrive.

With a rich heritage of academic achievement and character building, we prepare our students not just for examinations, but for life. Our experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and comprehensive curriculum ensure that each student receives the best possible education.
